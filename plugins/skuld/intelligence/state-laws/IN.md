# Indiana (IN) - Estate Planning Requirements

**Tier:** 2 (Standard Documentation)
**Property Type:** Common Law
**State Tax:** **Inheritance Tax** (only Class B & C beneficiaries)

## Execution Requirements

| Document | Witnesses | Notarization | Special Requirements |
|----------|-----------|--------------|---------------------|
| Will | 2 | Self-proving available | Standard execution |
| Trust | N/A | Required | Notary acknowledgment |
| POA | N/A | Required | Statutory form available |
| Healthcare | N/A | Required | Healthcare representative form |

## Key Features

### Inheritance Tax (Limited)
- Class A (spouse, descendants, ancestors): **Exempt**
- Class B (siblings, nieces, nephews): Graduated rates after $500 exemption
- Class C (others): Higher rates after $100 exemption
- Being phased out - check current status

### Transfer on Death Deed
- Indiana has TOD deed for real estate
- Effective for deaths after July 2009
- Revocable during lifetime

### Tenancy by the Entirety
- Available for real and personal property
- Creditor protection

## Elective Share
- Surviving spouse: 1/2 of net estate (no descendants) or 1/3 (with descendants)
- Can also claim 1/3 of real property (elective against will)

## Common Considerations
1. Check inheritance tax status (being phased out)
2. Use TOD deed for real estate
3. Healthcare POA not required but recommended
4. Probate simplified in Indiana
