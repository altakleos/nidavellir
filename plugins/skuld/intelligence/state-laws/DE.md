# Delaware (DE) - Estate Planning Requirements

**Tier:** 2 (Standard Documentation)
**Property Type:** Common Law
**State Tax:** None

## Execution Requirements

| Document | Witnesses | Notarization | Special Requirements |
|----------|-----------|--------------|---------------------|
| Will | 2 | Self-proving available | Standard execution |
| Trust | N/A | Required | Notary acknowledgment |
| POA | N/A | Required | Statutory form available |
| Healthcare | 2 | Not required | Advance directive |

## Key Features

### No State Death Taxes
- No estate tax
- No inheritance tax
- Favorable for wealth transfer

### Delaware Statutory Trust (DST)
- Favorable business entity and trust laws
- Popular for directed trusts
- Trust protector provisions
- Dynasty trust permitted (no rule against perpetuities for trusts)

### Asset Protection Trust
- Delaware allows domestic asset protection trusts
- Self-settled spendthrift trusts
- Favorable creditor protection laws

### Tenancy by the Entirety
- Available for all property (real and personal)
- Strong creditor protection
- Automatic right of survivorship

## Elective Share
- Surviving spouse: 1/3 of estate
- Or dower/curtesy (life estate in 1/3 of real property)

## Common Considerations
1. Favorable trust jurisdiction - consider Delaware situs
2. No rule against perpetuities for trusts
3. Directed trustee provisions
4. Corporate-friendly state (business interests)
