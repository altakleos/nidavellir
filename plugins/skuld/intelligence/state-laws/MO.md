# Missouri (MO) - Estate Planning Requirements

**Tier:** 2 (Standard Documentation)
**Property Type:** Common Law
**State Tax:** None

## Execution Requirements

| Document | Witnesses | Notarization | Special Requirements |
|----------|-----------|--------------|---------------------|
| Will | 2 | Self-proving available | Standard execution |
| Trust | N/A | Required | Notary acknowledgment |
| POA | N/A | Required | Statutory form available |
| Healthcare | 2 | Required | Durable POA for healthcare |

## Key Features

### No State Death Taxes
- No estate tax
- No inheritance tax
- Tax-friendly state

### Transfer on Death Deed
- Missouri has TOD deed (beneficiary deed)
- Avoids probate for real estate
- Revocable during lifetime
- Popular planning tool

### Tenancy by the Entirety
- Available for real and personal property
- Strong creditor protection

## Elective Share
- Surviving spouse: 1/2 of estate (no children) or 1/3 (with children)
- Or can take against will

## Common Considerations
1. TOD deed available for probate avoidance
2. Healthcare directive needs both witnesses AND notary
3. Missouri favors simplified probate
4. Independent administration available
