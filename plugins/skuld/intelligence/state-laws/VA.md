# Virginia (VA) - Estate Planning Requirements

**Tier:** 2 (Standard Documentation)
**Property Type:** Common Law
**State Tax:** None

## Execution Requirements

| Document | Witnesses | Notarization | Special Requirements |
|----------|-----------|--------------|---------------------|
| Will | 2 | Self-proving available | Standard or holographic |
| Trust | N/A | Required | Notary acknowledgment |
| POA | N/A | Required | Statutory form recommended |
| Healthcare | 2 | Recommended | Advance directive |

## Key Features

### No State Death Taxes
- No estate tax
- No inheritance tax
- Tax-friendly state

### Holographic Wills
- Virginia recognizes holographic wills
- Entirely in testator's handwriting
- Signed by testator

### Virginia Uniform Trust Code
- Modern trust provisions
- Trust protector allowed
- Decanting permitted

### Tenancy by the Entirety
- Available for real and personal property
- Strong creditor protection

### Transfer on Death Deed
- Virginia has TOD deed for real estate (since 2017)
- Avoids probate
- Revocable during lifetime

## Elective Share
- Surviving spouse: 1/3 or 1/2 of augmented estate
- Depends on whether there are descendants
- Includes nonprobate assets

## Common Considerations
1. No state death taxes
2. Holographic wills valid
3. TOD deed available
4. DC/MD coordination for metro area residents
5. Military families (large military presence)
