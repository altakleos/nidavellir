# West Virginia (WV) - Estate Planning Requirements

**Tier:** 2 (Standard Documentation)
**Property Type:** Common Law
**State Tax:** None

## Execution Requirements

| Document | Witnesses | Notarization | Special Requirements |
|----------|-----------|--------------|---------------------|
| Will | 2 | Self-proving available | Standard execution |
| Trust | N/A | Required | Notary acknowledgment |
| POA | N/A | Required | Durable POA |
| Healthcare | 2 | Not required | Living will or medical POA |

## Key Features

### No State Death Taxes
- No estate tax
- No inheritance tax
- Tax-friendly state

### Transfer on Death Deed
- West Virginia has TOD deed for real estate
- Avoids probate
- Revocable during lifetime

### Probate
- Supervised administration through county clerk
- Informal procedures available for smaller estates

## Elective Share
- Surviving spouse: varies based on circumstances
- 1/3 of net estate if descendants
- 1/2 if no descendants
- Plus allowances

## Common Considerations
1. No state death taxes
2. TOD deed available for real estate
3. Rural property - timber and mineral rights
4. Consider multi-state planning (VA, PA, OH borders)
