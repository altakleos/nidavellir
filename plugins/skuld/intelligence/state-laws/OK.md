# Oklahoma (OK) - Estate Planning Requirements

**Tier:** 2 (Standard Documentation)
**Property Type:** Common Law
**State Tax:** None

## Execution Requirements

| Document | Witnesses | Notarization | Special Requirements |
|----------|-----------|--------------|---------------------|
| Will | 2 | Self-proving available | Standard or holographic |
| Trust | N/A | Required | Notary acknowledgment |
| POA | N/A | Required | Statutory form available |
| Healthcare | 2 | Not required | Advance directive |

## Key Features

### No State Death Taxes
- No estate tax
- No inheritance tax
- Tax-friendly state

### Holographic Wills
- Oklahoma recognizes holographic wills
- Entirely in testator's handwriting
- Signed by testator

### Transfer on Death Deed
- Oklahoma has TOD deed for real estate
- Avoids probate
- Revocable during lifetime

### Tenancy by the Entirety
- Available for real and personal property
- Married couples only

## Elective Share
- Surviving spouse: 1/2 of property acquired during marriage
- Plus homestead rights

## Common Considerations
1. Holographic wills valid but formal will recommended
2. TOD deed available for real estate
3. Oil/gas/mineral rights - special planning
4. Tribal land considerations in some areas
