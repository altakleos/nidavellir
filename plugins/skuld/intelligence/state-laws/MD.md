# Maryland (MD) - Estate Planning Requirements

**Tier:** 2 (Standard Documentation)
**Property Type:** Common Law
**State Tax:** **Estate Tax** ($5M) + **Inheritance Tax** (non-spouse/lineal)

## Execution Requirements

| Document | Witnesses | Notarization | Special Requirements |
|----------|-----------|--------------|---------------------|
| Will | 2 | Self-proving available | Standard execution |
| Trust | N/A | Required | Notary acknowledgment |
| POA | 2 | Required | Both witnesses AND notary |
| Healthcare | 2 | Not required | Advance directive |

## Key Features

### Estate Tax
- Exemption: $5 million (fixed, not indexed)
- Maximum rate: 16%
- Decoupled from federal

### Inheritance Tax
- **Exempt**: Spouse, children, parents, grandparents, grandchildren, siblings
- **Taxable**: Others at 10%
- Applies to MD residents and MD property

### Tenancy by the Entirety
- Available for real and personal property
- Strong creditor protection

## Elective Share
- Surviving spouse: 1/2 of net estate (no children) or 1/3 (with children)
- Plus $5,000 minimum
- Can also claim 1/3 of property passing outside will

## Common Considerations
1. Both estate AND inheritance tax possible (non-lineal beneficiaries)
2. POA requires both witnesses AND notary
3. DC/VA suburb planning - coordinate multi-state
4. Credit for inheritance tax against estate tax
