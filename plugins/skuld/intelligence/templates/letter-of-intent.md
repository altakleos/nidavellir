# Letter of Intent to Special Needs Trust Trustee

## Template Overview

A Letter of Intent (LOI) is a non-legal companion document to a Special Needs Trust. It provides essential information about the beneficiary that cannot be captured in legal documents.

**Purpose**: Guide future trustees, caregivers, and advocates in understanding and caring for the beneficiary when the primary caregivers are no longer available.

**Legal Status**: Not legally binding, but critically important for quality of care.

---

## Document Template

```
LETTER OF INTENT
FOR
[BENEFICIARY_FULL_NAME]

Prepared by: [PARENT/GUARDIAN NAME(S)]
Date: [DATE]

This letter provides information about [BENEFICIARY_NAME] to guide future
caregivers, trustees, and advocates. While not legally binding, this document
reflects our intimate knowledge of [BENEFICIARY_NAME]'s needs, preferences,
and the life we envision for [him/her/them].

We ask that anyone involved in [BENEFICIARY_NAME]'s care read this letter
carefully and honor our wishes to the extent possible.
```

## Template Usage Instructions

### For the Generator Agent

When generating a Letter of Intent:

1. **Pre-populate known fields** from client profile:
   - Beneficiary name and DOB
   - Parent/guardian names
   - State of residence
   - Trust information
   - Known medical conditions

2. **Format as fillable template** with clear instructions

3. **Include reminder**:
```
IMPORTANT: This Letter of Intent should be:
- Updated annually at minimum
- Reviewed when circumstances change
- Stored with your estate planning documents
- Copied to all trustees, guardians, and advocates
- NOT kept secret - share with those who need it
```

4. **Generate in markdown format** for easy editing

### Output Format

```yaml
document_type: letter-of-intent
beneficiary: "[Name]"
document_content: |
  [Full LOI template with pre-populated fields]

instructions:
  - "Complete all sections with as much detail as possible"
  - "Update annually or when circumstances change"
  - "Share copies with trustees and caregivers"
  - "Store with your estate planning documents"

related_documents:
  - "Special Needs Trust"
  - "Guardianship/Conservatorship papers"
  - "ABLE account information"
```

## Review Checklist

Before finalizing Letter of Intent:
- [ ] All contact information current
- [ ] Medical information complete and accurate
- [ ] Daily routine reflects current reality
- [ ] Communication strategies clearly explained
- [ ] Financial accounts and contacts listed
- [ ] Vision section completed thoughtfully
- [ ] Date signed and witnesses noted
- [ ] Copies distributed to key people
