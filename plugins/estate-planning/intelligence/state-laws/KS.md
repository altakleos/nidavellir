# Kansas (KS) - Estate Planning Requirements

**Tier:** 2 (Standard Documentation)
**Property Type:** Common Law
**State Tax:** None

## Execution Requirements

| Document | Witnesses | Notarization | Special Requirements |
|----------|-----------|--------------|---------------------|
| Will | 2 | Self-proving available | Standard execution |
| Trust | N/A | Required | Notary acknowledgment |
| POA | N/A | Required | Statutory form available |
| Healthcare | 2 | Not required | Declaration or durable POA |

## Key Features

### No State Death Taxes
- No estate tax
- No inheritance tax
- Tax-friendly state

### Transfer on Death Deed
- Kansas has TOD deed for real estate
- Avoids probate
- Revocable during lifetime

### Homestead Protection
- Strong homestead protection (160 acres rural, 1 acre urban)
- Unlimited value
- Must be occupied

## Elective Share
- Surviving spouse: 1/3 of augmented estate (marriage under 5 years)
- Up to 50% for marriages of 15+ years
- Based on UPC model

## Common Considerations
1. TOD deed available for probate avoidance
2. Strong homestead protection
3. UPC-based elective share (length of marriage matters)
4. Agricultural and ranch property succession
