# Rhode Island (RI) - Estate Planning Requirements

**Tier:** 2 (Standard Documentation)
**Property Type:** Common Law
**State Tax:** **Estate Tax** ($1.774M exemption)

## Execution Requirements

| Document | Witnesses | Notarization | Special Requirements |
|----------|-----------|--------------|---------------------|
| Will | 2 | Self-proving available | Standard execution |
| Trust | N/A | Required | Notary acknowledgment |
| POA | N/A | Required | Statutory form available |
| Healthcare | 2 | Not required | Durable POA for healthcare |

## Key Features

### Estate Tax
- Exemption: $1.774 million (2025 - indexed for inflation)
- Maximum rate: 16%
- Decoupled from federal
- Lower exemption than federal

### Small State
- Probate through local probate courts
- Each city/town has probate court
- Procedures can vary by locality

## Elective Share
- Surviving spouse: life estate in all real property
- Or $75,000 outright
- Can also claim personal property allowance

## Common Considerations
1. $1.774M exemption lower than federal - tax planning needed
2. Indexed for inflation (check current year)
3. Small state - coordinate with MA and CT
4. Local probate courts - procedures vary
